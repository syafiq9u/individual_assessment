MY EDUCATION JOURNEY

For this page, I will explain my education journey from I was in primary school until today, continuing my Bachelor of Finance.

PRIMARY SCHOOL

After finishing my kindergarten lesson, I continued studying for my primary level education in Sekolah Kebangsaan Sultan Ismail (1). The school is located at Lorong Sekolah Ismail 1, Bandar Kota Bharu, 15150 Kota Bharu, Kelantan. During my study in this school, I was elected as a prefect from the 3rd Grade until the end of 6th Grade. In addition, during my study, I also was active in participating in yearly school sports event competitions, joining several competitions. At the end of my study, I managed to 4'A 1'B for the UPSR.


HIGH SCHOOL

Furthermore, after successfully finishing my primary education level study, I continue my study at my high school in Maktab Sultan Ismail. The school is located at Jalan Telipot, Maktab Sultan Ismail, 15150 Kota Bharu, Kelantan. During my study in this school, I was actively involved in curriculum activities. The activities that I typically joined is marching band, orchestra, culture and arts events, sports and many others. At the end of my study, I managed to 5'A for the SPM.



DIPLOMA

Next, after successfully finished my study at the high school level, I have continued to further my study by taking a Diploma in Accountancy at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang. During my study in UiTM Machang, I have joined various activities from the first day until the end of the day. In addition, I have managed to finish my Diploma on track, which was 3 years.
