MY EXPERIENCE JOURNEY

For this page, I will explain all of the experiences that I have been through since I was a kid until today.


1st EXPERIENCE

During the mid-semester break in Diploma, I volunteered to join with UiTM Machang Unit Kebudayaan to experience myself giving charity to the homeless people around the Kuala Lumpur city centre. This mission was collaborating between UiTM Machang and the NGO named DATH Charity. The NGO commonly will run their mission every week to serve the homeless that are needed. In this mission, we were given a bunch of essential supplies such as a blanket, food, a healthcare kit, and many more to distribute to the homeless people who truly needed it. We can give the supplies to around 300 homeless people consisting of children, adults, and elders. Click on the link below to visit DATH Charity on Instagram.

Visit Dath Charity On Instagram

2nd EXPERIENCE

During my high school, I have joined numerous competitions involving in Marching Competition. One of the biggest competitions I'd joined was the Kuala Lumpur International Youth Marching Band Competition 2015, held at Dataran Merdeka. This competition was organized by the Multi Empire Alliance, a company that actively organized marching band competitions around Malaysia since 2007. This KLIYMB competition was joined by several countries from the Asia countries such as Vietnam, Indonesia and Taiwan. Our school joined the Street Parade category for this competition and successfully managed to get Silver Award.

3rd EXPERIENCE

In 2019, during my Diploma at UiTM Machang, I joined the Unit Kokurikulum to participate in the Malaysia Book of Record, The Most Number of Palm Hand Stamped On A Wall Simultaneously Programme. This programme was organized by the Main UiTM Shah Alam campus in collaboration with the Jabatan Kebajikan Masyarakat. This programme was involved 4,000 volunteers from UiTM students from around the country and also the communities. UiTM and JKM managed to break the Malaysia Book of Record after successfully hand stamped on a wall simultaneously for a total of 130,585 murals.

4th EXPERIENCE

As I mentioned earlier, during my high school. I was actively involved in the marching band activities. Apart from joining the KLYIMB 2015 competition, I also managed to enter another marching band competition called National Band Competition (NATCOMP) in 2012 at Kuala Terengganu. The Malaysia National Band Competition (NATCOMP) is Malaysia's Premier Band Competition. It showcases the best of the best Bands in Malaysia for the coveted National Champion. Jointly organized by Persatuan Pancaragam Malaysia, Ministry of Education Malaysia and Z.I Production Sdn Bhd, the NATCOMP is also supported by Drum Corps Europe (DCE). In this competition, our school successfully managed to get second place.
